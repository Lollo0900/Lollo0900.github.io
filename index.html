research

Six peer-reviewed papers spanning high-energy theory and machine learning, ~60 citations total. Full record on INSPIRE (2711158).

A Pathway to Decay and Fission of Orthosymplectic Quiver Theories arXiv 2412.15202

Introduces an algorithm that derives the Coulomb-branch Hasse diagram of orthosymplectic 3d quiver gauge theories, validating it against class-S constructions and using it to uncover new Higgsing patterns in 6d orbi-instanton theories.

Detecting Homeomorphic 3-manifolds via Graph Neural Networks arXiv 2409.02126 · cs.LG

Reframes the homeomorphism problem for graph-manifolds — usually solved via super-polynomial-time graph moves — as a supervised learning task, benchmarking several GNN architectures on plumbing-graph pairs.

Higgs Branch of 6D (1,0) SCFTs and Little String Theories with Dynkin DE-type SUSY Enhancement arXiv 2406.02670

Details Higgs-branch RG flows for a class of 6d theories that enhance to (2,0) SCFTs of DE type, deriving magnetic quivers via a type-IIA brane picture and proposing a general "slice subtraction" rule for the foliation structure.

Unravelling T-Duality: Magnetic Quivers in Rank-zero Little String Theories arXiv 2312.12510

Derives magnetic quivers for rank-zero little string theories via brane systems, maps their Higgs-branch Hasse diagrams, and uses the resulting 2-group structure constants to conjecture new T-dual pairs.

Higgs Branch of Heterotic Little String Theories: Hasse Diagrams and Generalized Symmetries arXiv 2312.05306

Studies Higgs branches of heterotic little string theories via magnetic quivers and quiver subtraction, mapping Hasse diagrams of RG flows and using them to identify candidate T-dual families.

Sixth publication and complete citation record on INSPIRE.
